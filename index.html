FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

Got questions about your orthodontic treatment? We've got answers. If you don't find what you're looking for, feel free to WhatsApp us.

Do braces hurt?
Placing braces is completely painless. You may feel mild pressure or soreness for 2–3 days after fitting or adjustment as your teeth begin to shift — this is normal and easily managed with over-the-counter pain relief. Most patients adjust quickly and feel no discomfort afterward.
How long does clear aligner treatment take?
Treatment time depends on the complexity of your case, typically ranging from 6 to 18 months. Simple corrections may finish faster, while more involved bite corrections take longer. Dr. Nasir will give you a personalised timeline after your consultation and digital scan.
At what age should my child first see an orthodontist?
The American Association of Orthodontists recommends a first orthodontic check-up by age 7, even if all baby teeth haven't fallen out yet. Early evaluation helps catch bite and jaw growth issues while they're easiest to correct, though many patients also begin treatment successfully as teens or adults.
Do I need to wear a retainer after treatment?
Yes. Teeth naturally tend to shift back over time, so a retention phase is essential to protect your results. We'll fit you with a custom fixed or removable retainer and guide you on how long and how often to wear it for lasting alignment.
What are your clinic timings and is Sunday available?
Yes! We're open on Sundays. Our timings are: Morning — 10 AM to 2 PM, Evening — 5 PM to 8 PM (Mon–Sat), and Sunday — 10 AM to 2 PM. We recommend booking an appointment via WhatsApp or calling ahead to secure your preferred time slot.
How do I book an appointment?
You can book an appointment in three easy ways: (1) Fill out the appointment form on this page — it'll open WhatsApp with your details pre-filled, (2) Message us directly on WhatsApp at +91 77808 12225, or (3) Call us at +91 77808 12225 during clinic hours.
Is the clinic hygienic and safe?
Absolutely. We follow strict infection control protocols — all instruments are autoclaved, surfaces are disinfected between patients, and single-use disposable items are standard. The safety of our patients and staff is our top priority at all times.
